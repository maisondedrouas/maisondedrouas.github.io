## 🛠️ Key Case Studies

### 1. Patented Trash Collection Mechanism for Unmanned Vessels
* **The Challenge:** Unmanned surface vessels required a highly efficient, reliable, and durable mechanism to clear marine debris in unpredictable aquatic environments.
* **The Action:** Designed and prototyped a novel trash collection mechanism using advanced CAD software. Applied rigorous Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles to streamline components, reducing production costs and lead times. Conducted extensive troubleshooting to resolve field issues.
* **The Result:** Successfully secured **International Patent No. WO 2024/168604 A1** (August 2024) and significantly enhanced fleet operational efficiency.

### 2. Fleet-Wide Structural Optimization & Weight Reduction
* **The Challenge:** Marine vessels and embedded cargo/unloading systems were constrained by structural weight, limiting battery life, payload capacity, and deployment ranges.
* **The Action:** Pioneered a systematic weight-reduction methodology across the structural components of the vessel fleet. Utilized COMSOL for finite element analysis (FEA) to identify over-engineered components without sacrificing structural integrity.
* **The Result:** Achieved a **20% weight reduction** across a fleet of 15+ vessels while maintaining a **0% part failure rate** in the field.

### 3. Remote Controlled 3-Axis Underwater Camera System
* **The Challenge:** Creating a highly reliable, solar-powered winch system capable of precise 3-axis movement for underwater cameras in fish farm environments.
* **The Action:** Designed the mechanical layout of the winch system to enable 3-axis movement of a solar dock and its underwater camera. Handled electro-mechanical calibration, sensor integration, and solar charging electronic maintenance.
* **The Result:** Delivered a fully functional, highly reliable remote camera system tailored for aquaculture asset monitoring.
